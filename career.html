Cleveland Cavaliers (2003–2010)

2003: Drafted as the #1 pick.

LeBron quickly became one of the best players in the NBA, winning Rookie of the Year.

Led the team to the NBA Finals in 2007.

Miami Heat (2010–2014)

Formed a superteam with Dwyane Wade and Chris Bosh.

Won back-to-back championships in 2012 and 2013.

Earned 2 Finals MVP awards.

Return to Cavaliers (2014–2018)

Returned to Cleveland with a mission to bring a title.

2016: Won the NBA Championship after coming back from 3–1 in the Finals.

This is considered one of the greatest moments in NBA history.

Los Angeles Lakers (2018–Present)

Joined the Lakers to continue his legacy.

2020: Won another NBA Championship.

2023: Became the all-time leading scorer in NBA history.
